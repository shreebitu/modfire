Overview

Macro Gamer is a versatile and advanced macro automation application explicitly built for PC users. Carefully designed by itoday, this software enables you to seamlessly create, manage, and execute complex macros—which are essentially sequences of predefined commands. By automating highly repetitive events and actions, Macro Gamer serves as an essential tool for boosting speed and efficiency in both competitive gaming and routine productivity tasks.

Core Features of Macro Gamer

⏺️

Record Complex Sequences

Accurately capture explicit mouse click events and keyboard presses simultaneously to construct intricate macro sequences flawlessly.

⌨️

Custom Key Bindings

Easily assign your recorded macro actions to specific keyboard keys or mouse buttons, drastically optimizing your gaming and work setups.

🔁

Continuous Looping Mode

Execute macros continuously without interruption as long as the assigned button is held down. Great for rapid-fire techniques or intense grinding.

🖱️

Precise Mouse Playback

Replay physical mouse movements and specific clicking patterns with exact precision to automate repetitive cursor transitions.

📁

Profile Backup & Sharing

Seamlessly extract or back up your customized macro settings locally, and effortlessly export your profiles to share them with other users.

⚙️

Advanced Remapping & Delays

Rapidly reconfigure keystrokes, define precise delay times between click events, rename your active macros, and personalize hardware inputs safely.
