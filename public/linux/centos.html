About CentOS Stream

CentOS Stream is a continuous-delivery distribution that serves as the upstream development platform for Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL).

  • Upstream RHEL: Get access to the features going into the next RHEL release first.
  • Ecosystem Integration: Tested tightly against the vast Red Hat hardware array.
