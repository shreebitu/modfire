About ShreeBitu.in

A cloud storage and file sharing platform where anyone can upload, manage, and share files online. Built with passion by a young developer from India.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide a simple, secure, and free cloud storage platform where anyone can store and share their digital files without complexity.

🌟

Our Vision

To become a reliable and easy-to-use file hosting platform that anyone can access and use from anywhere in the world.

💡

Our Values

Simplicity, transparency, and open-source collaboration. We believe technology should be accessible to everyone.
